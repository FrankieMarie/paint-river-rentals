About the Properties

Available to reserve year round, our 3-bedroom, 2 bath cabin and 2-bedroom, 2 bath cabin are the perfect places for a family-friendly getaway or golf outing with friends!

Our comfortable cabins located in Crystal Falls, Michigan, can accommodate up to 8 people in the 3 bedroom property, and 6 people in the 2-bedroom property. The spacious open room concept, electric fireplace, full kitchen, and large patio overlooking Paint River provide plenty of room for fun with family and friends.
